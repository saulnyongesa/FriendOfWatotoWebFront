About Us

Who We Are

Friends of Tiwatoto is a community-based organization founded in 2015 with the goal of improving the lives of people in the Tiwatoto region. We believe in the power of community-driven solutions to create lasting change.

Our organization brings together local leaders, volunteers, and partners to address the most pressing challenges facing our community, from education and healthcare to environmental sustainability and economic development.

Over the years, we've grown from a small group of passionate individuals to a movement that impacts thousands of lives each year.

Our Mission

Our Mission & Values

Community Empowerment

We believe in equipping community members with the tools and resources they need to create sustainable change from within.

Education for All

Providing access to quality education is at the heart of our mission, ensuring every child has the opportunity to learn and grow.

Environmental Stewardship

We're committed to protecting our natural resources and promoting sustainable practices for future generations.
